INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT JORDAN

Official name Jordan’s official name is the “Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan”
The Rose City The Rose City famous for its unique architecture carved directly into the rock face.
DeadSea The lowest point on Earth in terms of dry land is the shore of the Dead Sea in Jordan. The waters are extremely saline.
Independent Jordan was formally recognised by the UN as an independent sovereign kingdom in 1946
biblical Sites The Jordan River where Jesus was babtised by John the Baptist, Mount Nebo where Moses died, as well as the sinful cities of Sodom and Gomorrah.
National Dish Mansaf is the national dish in Jordan. This distinctive option for a meal is made of lamb cooked in a sauce with fermented dried yogurt and served with rice in a large platter
